Our Mission

Our primary mission is to deliver high-quality digital education content to those without access via satellites.

About Us

Founded on February 2nd, 2020, Starborn Space is a student-led initiative that has been working to develop a fleet of satellites tasked with providing people around the world with an opportunity to education. We strive to make education a possibility for everyone, regardless of location.

Technology

We plan on launching several Low-Earth Orbit (LEO) satellites to distribute content globally. Our goal is to provide the user with the best experience possible by harnessing LEO's high bandwidth and low latency capabilities. With our design, just one satellite can have an impact upon millions of people.
